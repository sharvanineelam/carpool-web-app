CarPool is a ride coordination platform designed to simplify shared travel between people moving in the same direction. The application enables drivers to publish available seats in their vehicles while passengers can discover rides that match their journey.

Many people travel daily for work, education and personal commitments. Often vehicles travel with empty seats while others search for reliable transportation. CarPool was designed to connect drivers and passengers through a simple and organized ride sharing platform.

Through CarPool, users can publish rides, explore available journeys and manage travel details efficiently. The platform focuses on improving travel coordination, reducing transportation costs and making everyday commuting easier.
